Definition:
Metaverse is a neogolism and describes "online" "universes" available via "VR". MMORPGs are an implementation of Metaverse-like-concept focusing on gameplay elements.
Across all four mediums, Metaverse is "virtual world" using "VR" technology. Academic papers focus on "human experience", virtual worlds, and virtual objects. News and social media focus on the economic and social factors involved in the metaverse.
